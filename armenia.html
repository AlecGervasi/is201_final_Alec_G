Here are my 4 favorite parts about Armenia!

  1. HISTORY

    • Armenia is one of the oldest countries in the world dating back to 2949 BC


    • Armenia is mentioned in the Bible and is actually where Noah's ark was thought to have landed.


    • Most Notably, Armenia is knwon to be the first country to officially accpept Christianity and as their official religion.


  2. FOOD         

    • Armenian food is very similar to countries near them as you will find very similar meals as mediterranian and eastern food.

    • There will also be some delicious Russian meals making their appearance.

    • You will find an abundance of fresh fruit, healthy vegitables, and delicious meats.


  3. LANGUAGE

    • Armenian is an ancient language. In fact it goes back to the 5th century making it one of the oldest languages still spoken today!


    • There are 39 separate letters which also makes it one of the largest alphabets and each one has their own unique sound.


    • There are 2 main dialects spoken in the world now, Eastern and Western.


  4. CULTURE 

    • Armenia is full of exciting Holidays! My most favorite one is Vardavar. A day where you just throw water over anyone you see!


    • Armenia also has a very exciting dance called Kochari. It has been danced since ancient times when men would go to war.


    • Trndez is also a very old Armenian tradition which involved buring large bonfires because it is said to determine if couples should get married, break winter frost, and provide fertile ground.
